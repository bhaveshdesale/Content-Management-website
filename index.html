Content Management Software

CMS: CMS stands for Content Management System. It is a software application or a set of tools that enables users to create, manage, and modify digital content on a website without requiring technical knowledge or coding skills.
A CMS simplifies the process of website creation and maintenance by providing a user-friendly interface for managing content. The primary purpose of a CMS is to separate content from design, allowing users to focus on creating and managing content while the CMS takes care of the underlying structure and presentation. This separation of concerns enables non-technical users to update and modify website content without affecting the overall design or structure of the site.
